Handvo

Ingredients:

    For Soaking & Grinding

  1. 1 cup regular rice
  2. ½ cup moong dal (mung lentils)
  3. 1 tablespoon tuvar dal (pigeon pea lentils)
  4. 1 tablespoon urad dal (split & husked black gram)
  5. enough water for soaking
  6. ½ cup Curd (yogurt)
  7. ¼ teaspoon baking soda

      8. For Paste

    1. 1 inch ginger or 1 teaspoon chopped ginger (adrak)
    2. 4 medium garlic cloves or 1 teaspoon chopped garlic (lahsun)
    3. 1 to 2 green chilies or 2 teaspoons chopped green chilies

        4. For Tempering

      1. 1 tablespoon oil
      2. ½ teaspoon mustard seeds
      3. 5 to 6 fenugreek seeds (methi dana)
      4. 1 pinch asafoetida (hing)
      5. 1 teaspoon chopped curry leaves
      6. 1 cup tightly packed grated bottle gourd (lauki or ghia or dudhi or opo squash)
      7. ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
      8. ½ teaspoon Coriander Powder
      9. ¼ cup chopped coriander leaves

          10. Other Ingredient

        1. salt as required
        2. 4 to 5 teaspoons oil for greasing pan
        3. white sesame seeds as required
